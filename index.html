Toggle Development is a web and software development agency based in Seattle, WA. We specialize in helping businesses in the real estate industry with everything web and software related.

Custom Web Development

We are a full-service shop, meaning we’ll help you from initial idea through final design and software development. All of our websites are mobile friendly, responsive and SEO optimized. We pride ourselves on the level of polish we deliver, while relentlessly focusing on what’s most important — driving forward your business goals.

Process Automation & Full Software Integration

We offer custom software development, helping you automate and streamline business processes helping you save time and money. This includes many services such as custom in-house software development, data scraping, task management systems, CRM integrations, database management marketing automation and more.

Project Roadmapping

You will walk away with a clear, in-depth project outline. A holistic project map detailing every step from initial design through product launch. This includes developing a timeline, identifying the largest risks (and how to mitigate them) along with defining the exact feature set and technologies required. When developing roadmaps, we adhere to Lean Startup Methodology (MVP) and Agile Development principals.

Toggle's Story

Toggle was founded in 2017 by Patrick Ellis after spending the prior five years helping tech startups in Silicon Valley and Seattle develop and scale their web applications and internal software, working as both a Software Engineer and Product Manager. Patrick’s dream was to combine his deep passion for building software with his passion and family background in real estate, thus the founding of Toggle!
